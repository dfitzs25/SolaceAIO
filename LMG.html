What is Linus Media Group?

Linus Media Group is a company which aims to provide its audience with informative videos that showcase new technologies or devices. Linus Media Group (LMG) has multiple youtube channels, their main channel being Linus Tech Tips. The other channels under LMG are TechLinked, Techquickie and ShortCircuit. All four of these channels have over 1 million subscribers, with Linus Tech Tips having the most out of all of them with over 12 million subscribers.

What Do They do?

Each LMG channel has their own purpose that is unique to that particular channel. Linus Tech Tips, LMG's top channel, is meant to teach about technology in an entertaining and easy to understand way (Linus Tech Tips, n.d). This channel also posts videos about how to build computers and the difference between different computer parts so that consumers can make a decision with useful information. Techquickie, LMG second largest channel, is similar to Linus Tech Tips however it focuses on general topics more than specific pieces of hardware (Techquickie, n.d). Their most popular videos are quickly explaining topics such as how exactly youtube works or the differences between the many different display cables. The third most popular channel (by subscribers) is TechLinked. This channel is for people who want a quick recap of the past few days of tech news along with some fun commentary (TechLinked, n.d). The newest LMG channel is ShortCircuit, this channel is all about unboxing the newest tech (ShortCircuit, n.d). This means that they review tech that comes pre made such as laptops, tablets or phones. During these unboxings they will tell the audience their general opinion of how the device is made such as build quality, specs and overall thoughts.

LMG also has a forum, linustechtips, which allows people to ask questions to the community. This forum can help people with pc building, hardware options, software and even their opinion on games. Impact

LMG has allowed the general public to easily understand the complexities of tech. They allow people to confidently know the difference between different hardware, different manufacturers and different software. This company has allowed for people interested in tech to easily understand and learn about old and new technologies being made and produced.
